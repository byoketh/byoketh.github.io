Hardmode, for Dummies

info

Prioritize Defensive modules for Descendants you take into hard mode content

Focus on defensive modules early. Maximize Increased HP, DEF, and Elemental Resistance (Antibody) mods. These will make progressing to Hard Mode easier.

ATK for Weaponsâ€‹

Boost your ATK over Crit or Weak Point Mods for better scaling. Upgrade Elemental Enhancement Mods until Hard Mode.

Preferred Reactor Typesâ€‹

Find missions that drop your preferred Reactor types and farm them. Skill damage comes from Reactors. Prioritize Reactors that boost your damaging skills. Here's a list of useful Reactors for various Descendants:

  • Lepic: Burning Mechanics (Fire/Tech)
  • Ajax: Materialized Mechanics (Non-Attribute/Tech)
  • Viessa: Frozen Mechanics (Chill/Tech)
  • Sharen: Tingling Mixture (Elec/Fusion)
  • Bunny: Tingling Singularity (Elec/Singular)
  • Freyna: Toxic Mechanics (Tox/Tech)
  • Blair: Burning Phase (Fire/Dimension)

Combat Tipsâ€‹

Sub-Attacksâ€‹

Use sub-attacks to knockdown everything but Bosses and Colossi, allowing free shots.

Grappling Hookâ€‹

Use the Grappling Hook against enemies with buffs like Unbreakable Defense to close the distance and take them out faster.

AoE Skillsâ€‹

Use AoE skills to break Boss Orbs quickly, even Ultimates. They help get bosses out of immunity phases faster.

Defensive Componentsâ€‹

For actual Hard Mode challenges like harder missions and later bosses, get purely defensive parts with HP, DEF, and Resistance stats.

Resource Containers

Break every resource container you see. Each zone has unique resources essential for crafting.

Solo Hard Mode Infiltrationsâ€‹

Early on in Hard Mode, regarding infiltrations; Do more outposts to build up rare materials, but find groups or stick to regular missions.

Encrypted Vaultsâ€‹

Use your Ecieve scan around holographic markers to locate Encrypted Vaults. If you hear a unique ping, follow it to the vault. Enzo makes opening Ultra Precise Vaults easier. Hard Mode vaults require Code Breakers, not Code Analyzers.
